Thank you for installing Pacific theme. Follow the guide below on how to set it up.

-----Installation Guide-----

To apply this theme, open "Preferences > Setting-User" and add these codes in the comma-separated list:

    "color_scheme": "Packages/Theme - Pacific/Pacific Dark.tmTheme",

    "theme": "Pacific Dark.sublime-theme"

---

For more detail, check out https://github.com/hrsetyono/theme_pacific
